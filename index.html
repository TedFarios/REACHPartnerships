Mission & Vision

Mission

Turning every healthcare relocation into education, data, and measurable community health impact.

Vision

A world where every move by a healthcare professional strengthens the communities they serve.

The Regenerative Loop

1

Relocation (KWHP)

Relocations generate voluntary partner donations and mobility data that seed institutional micro-grants.

2

Institution

Hospitals and programs define SDOH problems and nominate trainees to lead projects.

3

Data Science Education (Erdos)

Scholars train on real datasets; 25% of tuition is returned to REACH to replenish the pool.

4

Staffing (BagMask)

Graduates are placed into data roles; workforce data and donations feed the next cohort.

5

Lifestyle (WealthMD)

Aggregate lifestyle insights inform future relocation counseling and institutional planning.
